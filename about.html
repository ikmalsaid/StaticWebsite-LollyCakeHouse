About Me

From zero to hero. This defines Lolly Cake House aim to achieve success in this huge industry. Without great support from family and friends, Lolly Cake House would never been exists today. A big thank you to everyone who always give exceptional support and motivation till this day.

My finest recipes and ingredients coupled with outstanding bakery and pastry skills from my college and also a few years of work experience in this field allow Lolly Cake House to provide the best cakes, cupcakes and macaroons to my customers. For the record, most of my customers praised my cakes, cupcakes and macaroons for having a very unique taste and also a very creative and clean presentation.

Finally, Lolly Cake House always strive to achieve a greater success and also very eager to keep improving the products for the future. Thank you!
